## 图 3-1　improper 策略与 proper 策略诱导弧子图 A_μ 的对照(对照论文 Fig 1)

**绘制要点**:A side-by-side comparison of two directed arc-subgraphs A_μ over the SAME node set X={1,2} plus an absorbing terminal node t. LEFT panel titled 'improper μ': three nodes drawn as rounded rectangles — node '1' top-left, node '2' bottom-left, node 't' on the right. Directed arcs: 1 -> t (horizontal arrow to the right); 1 -> 2 (downward); 2 -> 1 (upward, forming a 2-cycle); plus a self-loop on node 1 labeled '(1,1)'. Annotate that this subgraph CONTAINS a cycle: caption under it '含环 1→2→1、自环;对手可在 1、2 间往返不达 t'. RIGHT panel titled 'proper μ̄': same three nodes in the same layout — node '1' top-left, node '2' bottom-left, node 't' right. Directed arcs: 1 -> t (horizontal, right); 2 -> t (going up-and-right to t) and/or 2 -> 1 -> t with NO back edge from 1 to 2. No cycles, no self-loops. Caption under it '无环,每个节点都在有限步到达 t'. Both panels use the same clean black directed arrows with arrowheads; node t may be drawn with a double border or shaded to mark it as terminal/absorbing. A short title at top: '弧子图 A_μ:improper vs proper'.

**中文 prompt**:

绘制一张教科书风格的扁平矢量示意图,白色背景,黑色文字标注,无三维、无照片质感。主题:同一张图上两个策略诱导的弧子图 A_μ 的左右对照。共用节点集 X={1,2} 加一个吸收终点 t(t 用双线边框或浅灰底色标记为终点)。左侧子图标题『improper μ(含环)』:三个圆角矩形节点——节点『1』在左上,节点『2』在左下,节点『t』在右侧;有向弧(带箭头):1→t(水平向右)、1→2(向下)、2→1(向上)构成 1↔2 二元环,并在节点 1 上画一个自环,标注『(1,1)』。左图下方说明文字:『含环 1→2→1、自环;对手可让系统在 1、2 间往返,不达 t』。右侧子图标题『proper μ̄(无环)』:相同的三个节点、相同布局——节点『1』左上、节点『2』左下、节点『t』右侧;有向弧:1→t(水平向右)、2→t(经节点 1 到 t 或直接斜向右上到 t),没有从 1 回到 2 的反向边,整张子图无任何环、无自环。右图下方说明文字:『无环,每个节点都在有限步到达 t』。两图都用统一的黑色实线箭头。顶部主标题:『弧子图 A_μ:improper 与 proper 对照(对照论文 Fig 1)』。线条清晰、标签清晰可读。

**English prompt**:

Clean flat vector illustration, textbook quality, white background, black text labels, no photorealism, no 3D, legible math symbols. Subject: a side-by-side comparison of two directed arc-subgraphs A_μ induced by two policies over the SAME node set X={1,2} plus an absorbing terminal node t (draw t with a double border or light-gray fill to mark it as terminal). LEFT subgraph titled 'improper μ (has cycle)': three rounded-rectangle nodes — node '1' at top-left, node '2' at bottom-left, node 't' at right; directed arcs with arrowheads: 1 -> t (horizontal, rightward), 1 -> 2 (downward), 2 -> 1 (upward) forming a 1<->2 two-cycle, plus a self-loop on node 1 labeled '(1,1)'. Caption beneath the left panel: 'contains cycle 1→2→1 and self-loop; the adversary can keep the system oscillating between 1 and 2, never reaching t'. RIGHT subgraph titled 'proper μ̄ (acyclic)': the same three nodes in the same layout — node '1' top-left, node '2' bottom-left, node 't' right; directed arcs: 1 -> t (horizontal, rightward) and 2 -> t (either via node 1 then 1 -> t, or directly up-right to t), with NO back edge from 1 to 2, so the whole subgraph is acyclic with no self-loops. Caption beneath the right panel: 'acyclic; every node reaches t in finitely many steps'. Use consistent solid black directed arrows in both panels. Top title: 'Arc-subgraph A_μ: improper vs proper (cf. paper Fig 1)'.

## 图 5-1　T_μ 不动点迭代的一维 cobweb 示意:R(X)-regular(收敛)对 R(X)-irregular(发散到 +∞)

**绘制要点**:A 2D cobweb / fixed-point iteration diagram with two overlaid (or two side-by-side) cases. Axes: horizontal axis labeled 'J(x)' (the value at a representative state x), vertical axis labeled '(T_μ J)(x)'. Draw the 45-degree diagonal line y = x as a thin gray dashed reference line, labeled 'y = x'. CASE 1 — R(X)-regular (convergent), drawn in blue: a roughly straight map line of slope LESS THAN 1 that crosses the diagonal exactly once at a finite fixed point; mark and label this intersection point 'J_μ(x)' (a solid dot). Overlay a cobweb staircase: starting from an initial guess J_0 on the x-axis, draw the vertical-then-horizontal staircase steps spiraling/converging inward toward the fixed point J_μ(x), with small arrowheads showing the direction of convergence; annotate 'slope < 1 ⇒ 收敛到有限不动点 J_μ(x)' (converges to finite fixed point). CASE 2 — R(X)-irregular / positive cycle (divergent), drawn in red: a map line that lies ENTIRELY ABOVE the diagonal (never intersects it), so there is no finite fixed point; overlay a cobweb staircase that steps progressively up and to the right, escaping toward the top-right corner, with arrowheads, and an upward arrow annotated '→ +∞ (发散)'. Include a small legend distinguishing the blue 'R(X)-regular (proper)' curve from the red 'R(X)-irregular (正环)' curve. Title at top: 'T_μ 不动点迭代的 cobweb 示意'. Keep it schematic and clean.

**中文 prompt**:

绘制一张教科书风格的扁平矢量示意图,白色背景,黑色坐标轴与文字,无三维、无照片质感,数学符号清晰可读。主题:固定策略算子 T_μ 的一维不动点迭代(cobweb)示意,对比两种情形。坐标轴:横轴标注『J(x)』(代表性状态 x 的值),纵轴标注『(T_μ J)(x)』。画一条 45 度对角参考线 y = x(细灰色虚线,标注『y = x』)。情形一——R(X)-regular(收敛),用蓝色:一条斜率小于 1 的近似直线映射,与对角线恰好相交于一个有限不动点,用实心圆点标出并标注『J_μ(x)』;叠加 cobweb 阶梯——从横轴上某初值 J_0 出发,画『先竖后横』的阶梯,逐步向内收敛到不动点 J_μ(x),阶梯上加小箭头表示收敛方向,旁注『斜率<1 ⇒ 收敛到有限不动点 J_μ(x)』。情形二——R(X)-irregular(正环,发散),用红色:一条始终位于对角线上方、与对角线无交点的映射直线(无有限不动点);叠加 cobweb 阶梯,逐级向右上方抬高、逃逸到右上角,加箭头,并用一个向上箭头标注『→ +∞(发散)』。右上角放一个小图例,区分蓝色『R(X)-regular(proper)』与红色『R(X)-irregular(正环)』两条曲线。顶部标题:『T_μ 不动点迭代的 cobweb 示意:收敛 vs 发散』。整体保持示意、简洁、线条清晰。

**English prompt**:

Clean flat vector illustration, textbook quality, white background, black axes and text labels, no photorealism, no 3D, legible math symbols. Subject: a 1D cobweb / fixed-point iteration diagram for the fixed-policy operator T_μ, contrasting two cases. Axes: horizontal axis labeled 'J(x)' (value at a representative state x), vertical axis labeled '(T_μ J)(x)'. Draw the 45-degree diagonal reference line y = x as a thin gray dashed line labeled 'y = x'. CASE 1 — R(X)-regular (convergent), in BLUE: an approximately straight map line with slope LESS THAN 1 that crosses the diagonal exactly once at a finite fixed point; mark this intersection with a solid dot labeled 'J_μ(x)'. Overlay a cobweb staircase starting from an initial value J_0 on the x-axis, drawing vertical-then-horizontal steps that converge inward to the fixed point J_μ(x), with small arrowheads indicating the convergence direction; annotate 'slope < 1 ⇒ converges to finite fixed point J_μ(x)'. CASE 2 — R(X)-irregular / positive cycle (divergent), in RED: a map line lying ENTIRELY ABOVE the diagonal (no intersection, hence no finite fixed point); overlay a cobweb staircase that steps progressively up and to the right, escaping toward the top-right corner with arrowheads, plus an upward arrow annotated '→ +∞ (diverges)'. Include a small legend distinguishing the blue 'R(X)-regular (proper)' curve from the red 'R(X)-irregular (positive cycle)' curve. Top title: 'Cobweb of T_μ fixed-point iteration: convergent vs divergent'. Keep it schematic and clean.

## 图 6-1　陷阱 gadget(节点 0=u、1=v、终点 2=t),展示名义捷径在对抗下成正环失效

**绘制要点**:A small directed-graph 'trap gadget' with exactly three nodes drawn as circles: node 'u' (=0) at left, node 'v' (=1) at lower-middle, node 't' (=2, terminal) at right (draw t with a double border / shaded as absorbing). The diagram must visually DISTINGUISH two kinds of successor using two line styles: (1) NOMINAL successor edges as SOLID green arrows, (2) ADVERSARIAL successor edges as DASHED red arrows. Edges to draw: From u, risky action a0: a SOLID green arrow u -> t labeled 'a0 nominal: 直达 t(便宜)' AND a DASHED red arrow u -> v labeled 'a0 对抗: →v'. From u, safe action a1: a SOLID black arrow u -> t labeled 'a1 safe: 直达 t(贵)'. From v, action b0: a SOLID green arrow v -> t labeled 'b0 nominal: 直达 t(便宜)' AND a DASHED red arrow v -> u labeled 'b0 对抗: →u'. The two dashed red adversarial edges u->v and v->u together form a CYCLE; HIGHLIGHT this positive cycle u → v → u prominently — e.g., draw a translucent red/orange highlight loop or a curved red band around the u<->v pair, with a bold label '正环 u→v→u (improper, J_μ = ∞)'. Add a legend box: solid green = 'nominal 后继(便宜)', dashed red = '对抗后继', solid black = 'safe 动作'. Add a short caption: 'nominal/bestcase baseline 选 a0/b0 → 对抗下成正环失效;VI 选 a1 直达 t → proper, J_μ 有限'. Title at top: '陷阱 gadget:名义捷径在对抗下成正环'.

**中文 prompt**:

绘制一张教科书风格的扁平矢量有向图示意,白色背景,黑色文字,无三维、无照片质感,标签清晰可读。主题:一个三节点『陷阱 gadget』。三个圆形节点:节点『u』(=0)在左,节点『v』(=1)在下方中间,节点『t』(=2,终点)在右(t 用双线边框或浅灰底标记为吸收终点)。必须用两种线型区分两类后继:(1)名义(nominal)后继 = 绿色实线箭头;(2)对抗(adversarial)后继 = 红色虚线箭头。需要画的弧:从 u 的风险动作 a0——一条绿色实线 u→t,标注『a0 nominal:直达 t(便宜)』;同时一条红色虚线 u→v,标注『a0 对抗:→v』。从 u 的安全动作 a1——一条黑色实线 u→t,标注『a1 safe:直达 t(贵)』。从 v 的动作 b0——一条绿色实线 v→t,标注『b0 nominal:直达 t(便宜)』;同时一条红色虚线 v→u,标注『b0 对抗:→u』。两条红色虚线对抗弧 u→v 与 v→u 共同构成一个环;请醒目地高亮这个正环 u→v→u——例如用半透明红/橙色环形高亮或围绕 u、v 的弧形色带,并加粗标注『正环 u→v→u(improper,J_μ = ∞)』。加一个图例框:绿色实线=『nominal 后继(便宜)』、红色虚线=『对抗后继』、黑色实线=『safe 动作』。底部说明文字:『nominal/bestcase baseline 在 u 选 a0、在 v 选 b0 → 对抗下成正环失效;VI 在 u 选 a1 直达 t → proper,J_μ 有限』。顶部标题:『陷阱 gadget:便宜的名义捷径在对抗下成正环失效』。

**English prompt**:

Clean flat vector directed-graph illustration, textbook quality, white background, black text labels, no photorealism, no 3D, legible symbols. Subject: a small three-node 'trap gadget'. Three circular nodes: node 'u' (=0) at left, node 'v' (=1) at lower-middle, node 't' (=2, terminal) at right (draw t with a double border / light-gray fill to mark it absorbing). The figure MUST visually distinguish two successor types via two line styles: (1) NOMINAL successor edges as SOLID GREEN arrows, (2) ADVERSARIAL successor edges as DASHED RED arrows. Edges to draw: From u, risky action a0 — a solid green arrow u -> t labeled 'a0 nominal: direct to t (cheap)' AND a dashed red arrow u -> v labeled 'a0 adversarial: → v'. From u, safe action a1 — a solid black arrow u -> t labeled 'a1 safe: direct to t (expensive)'. From v, action b0 — a solid green arrow v -> t labeled 'b0 nominal: direct to t (cheap)' AND a dashed red arrow v -> u labeled 'b0 adversarial: → u'. The two dashed red adversarial edges u->v and v->u together form a CYCLE; prominently HIGHLIGHT this positive cycle u → v → u — e.g., a translucent red/orange highlight loop or curved band around the u<->v pair, with a bold label 'positive cycle u→v→u (improper, J_μ = ∞)'. Add a legend box: solid green = 'nominal successor (cheap)', dashed red = 'adversarial successor', solid black = 'safe action'. Bottom caption: 'nominal/bestcase baseline picks a0 at u and b0 at v → becomes a positive cycle under adversary and fails; VI picks a1 at u (direct to t) → proper, finite J_μ'. Top title: 'Trap gadget: a cheap nominal shortcut becomes a positive cycle under adversary'.
